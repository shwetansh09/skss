Our Story

A Legacy Woven Into
Every Household

What began in 1685 as a humble trading post under Sitaram Jangilal Sahu Pratisthan has flourished across generations into one of the region's most trusted grocery destinations. The name of Mahesh Prasad Sahu became synonymous with quality, honesty, and community service.

Today, under Sharad Kumar Sahu & Bro., that same spirit lives on — refined with modern standards yet rooted in the values our ancestors built. We are not just a store; we are a relationship that spans centuries.
